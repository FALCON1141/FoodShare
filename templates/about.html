About FoodShare+

🌍 Our Mission

FoodShare+ is a community-driven platform dedicated to reducing food waste and helping people in need across Bangladesh. Every day, tons of edible food are wasted while many struggle to find meals. We aim to bridge that gap.

🤝 What We Do

We connect donors, volunteers, and communities through a simple system:

  • 🍱 Donors share surplus food
  • 🚚 Volunteers collect and deliver
  • ❤️ People in need receive support

🇧🇩 Why Bangladesh?

In Bangladesh, food waste and food insecurity exist side by side. FoodShare+ focuses on local communities, ensuring quick and efficient distribution where it matters most.

💡 Our Vision

We envision a Bangladesh where no food is wasted and no one sleeps hungry. With the power of community and technology, we aim to create a sustainable food-sharing ecosystem.
